Welcome to Music Faker!


Here are a few helpful tips to get you started:


Choosing Tracks
  • All numbered tracks are made to work with each other
  • Tracks like "Blues Rhythm One" and "Blues Drums One" go together like rock shows and hearing loss!
  • However, this isn't set in stone! Feel free to experiment as all tracks are in the same key and bpm

Playing The Tracks
  • It is advisable to only use the Master Play Button when listening to the full song
  • It runs off a timer so things get weird if you try to pause or rewind during playback
  • When working on a specific section, it's better to just use the play button associated with that section
  • These buttons are located right below the Tracking Area. Look for buttons labeled "Play One", "Play Two" etc.
Volume Control
  • Each Track has its own volume slider
  • This slider will control the volume for all sections of that Track
  • Contrary to popular belief, ten isn't always the right choice!
  • Sometimes for a Lead to really shine, the Drums, Rhythm and Bass might need to be turned down a bit
Jamming
  • Music Faker is perfect to play along with!
  • If you're looking to jam, all tracks are recorded in the key of C (a minor), at 120 bpm

Thank you for checking out our app! We here at the Music Faker Dev Team (both of us) hope you enjoy your experience!
